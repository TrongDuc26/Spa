OUR FOUNDER

BARBARA CLOSE


Inspired by childhood visits to apothecaries with her aunt in France, Barbara embarked on an exploration of natural healing at an early age. Barbara now holds a Master’s degree in Therapeutic Herbalism and credentials in esthetics, advanced massage and aromatherapy. Her expertise was tested when her mother was diagnosed with lung cancer. During that difficult time, Barbara used lemon balm tea to ease her mother’s anxiety, her own calendula cream to soothe her mother’s sensitized skin, and peppermint aromatherapy to quell her mother’s nausea.










BIRTH OF A BRAND

In 1995 Barbara opened Naturopathica Healing Arts Center & Spa in East Hampton to share her discoveries with a wider audience. Today, Barbara is regarded as a pioneer in the wellness movement and the leader in holistic skin care. Today, Naturopathica Holistic Health features a robust collection of skin and well-being products, three celebrated flagship spas in Manhattan and East Hampton, and an impressive roster of Spa Partners across the country, including Miraval, The Lake Austin Spa Resort, The Spa at Terranea, and Heyday Spas.


AUTHOR AND SPEAKER

From the beginning, Barbara sought to demonstrate the connection between inner wellness and outer beauty. To share her vast knowledge, Barbara has authored three books: Well Being (2000), Pure Skin: Holistic Beauty Basics (2005) and The Naturopathica Effect: A Holistic Approach to Skin Health (2018). Barbara is also a popular speaker and panelist at private and public events across the country, leveraging her knowledge as an esthetician, aromatherapist, and master herbalist to educate her listeners and readers on the benefits of her holistic approach to beauty.
